I can't edit the account information on an order request.

Account information as well as line items require an extra level of awareness to edit. Changing of this information could potentially result in the order being rerouted and the current user losing access to the request.

To select an account, scroll to the section above the line items and click on the button "Enable Modification". This will prompt you with a warning, confirm this action then the line items and accounting information will be editable.
